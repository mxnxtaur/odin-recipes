Creamy Mushrooms on Toast

Description

A delicious balanced breakfast that combines eggs and mushrooms with a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chopped mushrooms
  • 2 eggs
  • 150 ml of heavy cream
  • 10g of dijon mustard
  • 1 slice of sourdough toast
  • 10g of salt
  • 10g of pepper
  • Pea chutes for presentation

Steps

  1. Cook the mushrooms in a pan and remove when they are nicely browned and glistening.
  2. Pour in the heavy cream
  3. Pretend the recipe continues

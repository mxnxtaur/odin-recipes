Baklava

Description

A delicious Turkish desert that pairs well with coffee! Your taste buds are in for a sweet, nutty treatâ€”each layer is filled with ground pistachios, and after baking the entire pastry is soaked in sweet syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1kg ground pistachios
  • 17 sheets of philo dough
  • 250g of melted butter
  • 1 lemon
  • 500ml of water
  • 500g of sugar

Steps

  1. Add 500ml of water and 500 grams of sugar to a pot and bring the water to a slow simmer.
  2. Let the water simmer for 15 minutes or until consistency of the syrup leaves the back of a spoon coated.
  3. Begin layering the philo dough into a baking pan. Stack three sheets before sprinkling an even amount of pistachios on this layer.
  4. Layer another philo sheet on top of the pistachios and sprinkle pistachios onto this new layer.
